Garri

Soaked Garri 'cereal' is definitely a must try for anyone visiting Nigeria for the first time but if you don't intend visiting Nigeria soon, this recipe will take you on an amazing Journey to my home country. Please join me ?

Ingredients

  • Garri
  • Sweetner
  • Groundnuts
  • water
  • Ice Cubes

Steps

  1. Like traditional cereal, it's super easy to make.
  2. Put some Garri in a bowl, pour in some water way above the level of the Garri.
  3. Sieve our the floating particles.
  4. Add more water, some ice cubes, sugar / sweetener, groundnuts and enjoy.
